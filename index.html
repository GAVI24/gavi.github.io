Welcome to Gayaza Vocational Institute - GAVI!. We are training Vocational Skills to go and serve in a holistic manner. It is our calling to give the learners Competent Skills training together with Life Skills they need to become competitive professionals and responsible citizens.


All our Courses follow the Directorate of Industrial Training - DIT Syllabi. We add on those disciplines we believe can give a difference to our graduates when they are out in the field.


At Gayaza Vocational Institute God comes first. We believe that we are here to serve and Worship God therefore we put Him first in all our training Activities.
